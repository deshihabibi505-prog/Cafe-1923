About Us

Cafe 1923 is a neighborhood coffee shop in Hamtramck, MI. We serve well-made coffee, tea, and simple food in a calm, welcoming space filled with books, warm wood, and comfortable seating.

Why People Love Us

  • Friendly staff and relaxed atmosphere
  • Indoor and outdoor seating
  • Free Wi-Fi
